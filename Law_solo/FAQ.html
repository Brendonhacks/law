General Inquiries

Is my information confidential before I hire you?

Yes. We maintain strict confidentiality for all preliminary inquiries in accordance with professional ethics. However, please be advised that a formal solicitor-client relationship only starts after an engagement letter has been duly executed by both parties.

Where are your primary chambers located?

Our principal office is situated in the heart of Abuja, providing proximity to the superior courts of record and regulatory administrative bodies.

Consultations

How do I schedule a consultation?

You may schedule a session by clicking the 'Contact Us' button in the navigation header or by visiting our contact page. Our secretariat will respond within 24 hours to confirm a time that aligns with the Counsel's diary.

What are your consultation fees?

Consultation fees vary depending on the complexity of the matter and the expertise required. We maintain a transparent billing structure which will be communicated to you prior to the confirmation of your appointment.

Practice Specifics

Do you take cases outside of Abuja?

Yes. While our base is in the FCT, we represent clients in superior courts across Nigeria. Interstate practice is fully supported, and we have established networks to facilitate efficient legal service delivery nationwide.

Which areas of law do you specialize in?

Our expertise is centered on high-stakes litigation, corporate advisory, and constitutional law. We provide tailored advocacy for institutional partners and private individuals requiring rigorous legal intervention.
